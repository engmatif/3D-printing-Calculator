Built for Accurate
3D Printing Pricing

PrintQuote was created to solve one of the most common problems in 3D printing: inaccurate pricing. Most makers and small print businesses either undercharge, overcharge, or rely on rough estimates that ignore machine wear, electricity usage, material waste, and operational costs.

Why This Exists

Many online calculators are either too simple for real manufacturing use or too complicated for everyday workflows. PrintQuote aims to create a balance between speed, usability, and professional-grade pricing logic.

The goal is to help makers, freelancers, print farms, and manufacturing startups estimate production costs more accurately and make smarter business decisions.

Core Principles

Accurate Pricing

Material cost alone is not enough. Real manufacturing includes electricity, maintenance, risk, and operational overhead.

Engineering Focus

The calculator is designed with a manufacturing mindset, not just as a hobby tool.

Fast Workflow

Professional tools should remain fast and simple to use during real production workflows.

Scalable Platform

G-code parsing, smarter pricing systems, and print-farm workflow tools are all part of the roadmap.

Future Direction
